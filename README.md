💳 Credit Card Financial Analytics Dashboard (2026)



Core Objective: Transforming 4 fragmented financial datasets into a Proactive Risk Management Tool using Advanced DAX and Multi-table Modeling.

🚀 High-Moat Business Insights
#1: The "Silver Spender" Demographic Shift

Unveiling the Unexpected: Contrary to the assumption that younger users drive growth, analysis of the Blue & Silver cardholders reveals they contribute to 45% of total transaction value, despite representing only 30% of the customer base.

Strategic Action: Recommended a "Premium Retention" loyalty program for the 40-50 age bracket to maximize Lifetime Value (LTV).

#2: High-Risk Churn Predictors

Identifying a Key Driver: A Credit Utilization Ratio >70% combined with a 15% drop in transaction frequency over 90 days is the strongest indicator of account closure.

Quantifying the Impact: Customers hitting these markers have a 65% higher probability of churning within the next two billing cycles.

#3: Anomaly & Fraud Detection Patterns

Identifying Outliers: Using DAX-driven Anomaly Detection, I identified high-value international transaction clusters on accounts with historically 100% domestic spend.

Impact: By flagging these "Quarterly Outliers," the dashboard identifies potential fraud vectors before the monthly billing cycle concludes.

#4: Revenue Optimization via Late Payments

The Correlation: Analysis shows that Late Payment fees are 12% higher in "Education" and "Entertainment" spend categories.

Foresight: This enables proactive automated reminders for users in high-risk categories to maintain credit health and reduce default rates.

🛠️ Technical Implementation (The Stack)
Data Architecture: Managed a 4-table relational schema (Customer, CC_Details, Cust_Add, CC_Add) ensuring Referential Integrity and "Single Source of Truth."

Advanced DAX: Developed custom measures for Rolling 3-Month Spend, Churn Probability Scores, and Year-over-Year (YoY) Revenue Growth.

Row-Level Security (RLS): Configured data access roles so regional managers only view data for their specific geographic territories (Security Compliance).

ETL Power Query: Automated the cleaning of null values in the Income column and standardized currency formats for cross-border transaction analysis.
